About REQS (dot) TECH

REQS.tech is the digital workshop of a creatively technical mind. It’s where ideas get built, broken, tested, versioned, scrapped, and rebuilt better. This isn’t a portfolio — it’s a proof-of-concept for the brain behind the build.

You’ll find experimental code, mildly unhinged documentation, weirdly effective workflows, and the occasional beautiful workaround. Because here, “a workaround is not a solution” — but it might just lead to one.

Built with wielded chaos, fierce curiosity, and an ongoing obsession with making things that don’t just work — they resonate.
