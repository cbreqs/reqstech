Business Card Case Study

This case study walks through the creation and refinement of the REQS.TECH business card—beginning with layout experiments, visual hierarchy, and branding goals.

Design Intent

The core idea was to balance professional clarity with personality. Typography choices, spacing, and color modes all reflect the brand's values: technical creativity, adaptability, and structured problem-solving.

Layout Iterations

Multiple versions were developed and tested for both the front and back, ensuring cohesion with the site's visual identity. The final layout subtly communicates purpose through shape, flow, and restraint.
